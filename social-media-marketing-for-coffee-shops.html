Why Every Business Needs A Website

Last updated Dec. 13, 2019


According to this report, 63% of shopping starts online. It means either user purchases online or from a brick-and-mortar shop, the first thing is that he searches for the item online. And as per this report, 47% of online viewers, visit the company website first. So, in this online era, a business should have a website to capture a lot of audience.


GIVES MORE CONTROL AT HOW YOU PRESENT BUSINESS IN THE ONLINE WORLD


The website gives you control over how you want to present your company and products to the online world. It allows you to have your own design, content, and strategies to influence the audience. In contrast, social-media platform bound you to follow their rules and design to present yourself to the online world.

The website can have a company blog as well, through which business owners can convey their mission and personality more effectively than print-adds and mail-vouchers.


ADDS MORE CREDIBILITY TO YOUR BUSINESS


The online customers are a little skeptic, so having a good website helps to build credibility as a legitimate business.

The website gives a very strong impression on the customers.


SAVES YOUR TIME


A good website saves a lot of time for the business owners. For example, website has a section of FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions - by customers) answers to most common queries that customers have about your company and products. Online order placement also boosts time-management. You can generate reports to analyze the daily progress of your business without asking employees/customers for every detail.
